**Scenario 2 – 5G Advanced / 5.5G Leadership**

HUAWEI and H3G have a strategic opportunity to co\-launch a premium, value\-added tariff package powered by HUAWEI FWA devices. By utilizing device\-side Quality of Service \(QoS\) technology and the APP Turbo feature, this partnership targets high\-value, heavy\-data segments—such as competitive gamers and remote workers—by offering on\-demand speed acceleration for specific applications.

For Consumers: Premium users demand rock\-solid connection stability and are willing to pay a premium for it. APP Turbo delivers a superior user experience by prioritizing critical traffic: Gamers: Reduced latency and jitter via dedicated game acceleration. Remote Workers: Seamless, buffer\-free video conferencing and data transfers.

For H3G: ARPU Growth &amp; Differentiation. This initiative directly aligns with H3G’s flexible tariff and QoS technology strategies. While H3G already offers targeted packages for youth, travelers, and competitor switchers, this package addresses an untapped, high\-value segment. Drives ARPU \(Average Revenue Per User\) growth, boosts customer satisfaction, and differentiates H3G's portfolio.

For HUAWEI: This feature is already built into HUAWEI’s FWA ecosystem and has proven commercial success across Orange OpCos.

Strategic Impact: Demonstrates competitive differentiation against other hardware vendors without requiring heavy, net\-new R&amp;D investment.

Required Investment &amp; Localization: To launch successfully, the partnership requires localized optimization rather than deep technical overhauls: App Customization: Aligning and tailoring the APP Turbo whitelist to match local OpCo target segments and country\-specific popular applications \(e.g., specific regional gaming servers or enterprise tools\).

Risk: \(1\) Without 5G Core integration in Europe, QoS is restricted to the device side. Acceleration limits exist without network\-side enforcement. \(2\) Promoted as a "reliable, high\-speed" premium tier, any instability poses a high reputation risk for H3G and strict TA demands for HUAWEI.

Estimated profitability \(take Italy as example\): assume 5% of the new users \(based on current size estimation\) opt into the Turbo package with +3 EURO per month \(€19.99 for normal package while €22.99 for Turbo\), the pure premium from this group will be €270k per year. Consider existing users switch and proposition scaling up new users size, the profitability add\-on will exceeds \$0.5M easily without extra OpEx.
