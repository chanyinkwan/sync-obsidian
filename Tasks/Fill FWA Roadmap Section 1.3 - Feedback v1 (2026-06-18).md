### FWA-specific data — source scan

> Conclusion: you were right — the customer's **FWA-specific ARPU / churn / NPS are essentially not open-source.** But FWA **market / lines / share** are available from regulators, more precise than the group fiscal.

Best sources by purpose:
- **AGCOM (Italy regulator) — best.** Quarterly *Osservatorio sulle Comunicazioni* gives FWA lines by operator & technology → derive WindTre's FWA subscribers and share.
- **RTR (Austria regulator) — Telekom Monitor.** Austrian broadband / FWA data (3 / Drei).
- **Analysts:** Omdia, Ericsson Mobility Report, Analysys Mason (market sizing/forecasts, mostly paywalled).
- **Opensignal / Ookla:** FWA experience / speed, not subscribers.
- **Operator PR:** WindTre / Drei coverage & launch regions (not KPIs).

Key findings (matter for the roadmap itself):
- **WindTre is a small FWA player in Italy: ~76,000 FWA lines (AGCOM, Q1 2024), only 0.3% of its SIMs.** Italy FWA is led by EOLO (650k+), TIM, Fastweb, Vodafone, Linkem.
- Italy FWA ≈ 11% of broadband, 2.1M+ connections, ~+8% YoY.
- 3 Austria has 5G FWA (consumer + business) but subscriber numbers aren't public → use RTR.
- **Strategic implication:** H3G's FWA is still nascent — that itself is §1.4 pain-point / cooperation-opportunity material, and explains why this FWA business-development roadmap exists.

§1.3 sourcing conclusion:
- **Market Share / FWA subscribers / growth** → AGCOM (IT) + RTR (AT); fillable and FWA-specific.
- **ARPU / Churn / NPS (FWA level)** → not public → use group / 3 Group Europe proxy (labelled) or get internal data from Ziyi.
